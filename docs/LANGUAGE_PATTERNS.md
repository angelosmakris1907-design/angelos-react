# Angelos Language Patterns

## Add Task Words
These should all mean "create a task":
- add
- write
- put
- note down
- remember
- remind me
- tell me
- do

## Reminder Time Phrases
- today
- tomorrow
- later
- later today
- this evening
- at 5
- at 5 pm
- in 10 minutes
- in 2 hours
- in 2 hours and 30 minutes

## Note Phrases
These should create a note, not a task:
- remember that...
- note that...
- write down that...

## Examples

"Add call George tomorrow"
→ Task: call George
→ Date: tomorrow

"Remind me in 10 minutes to check the oven"
→ Task: check the oven
→ Reminder: in 10 minutes

"Remember that George prefers morning appointments"
→ Note: George prefers morning appointments

"Tell me later to send the email"
→ Task: send the email
→ Time: later today

"Do the laundry at 5 pm"
→ Task: the laundry
→ Time: 5 pm
